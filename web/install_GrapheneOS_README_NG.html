Taƙaitaccen bayani

Wannan ma'ajiyar tana ɗauke da Bash script da aka tsara don sarrafa aikin shigar GrapheneOS a kan kowace na'urar Google Pixel. Script ɗin yana aiwatar da sabunta tsarin da ake buƙata, shigar da fakitin da ake buƙata kuma yana jagorantar mai amfani yayin aikin flashing.


Sharuɗɗan farko

Kafin gudanar da script, tabbata kana da waɗannan:

* Komfuta mai rarraba Linux bisa Debian (misali, Ubuntu).
* Na'urar Pixel.
* Kebul na USB don haɗa na'urar zuwa kwamfuta.
* Sanin yadda ake amfani da tashar umarni (terminal).


Abubuwan da script ɗin ke yi:

1. Sabunta da haɓaka fakitin tsarin
2. Shigar da fakitoci masu mahimmanci
3. Sauke sigar GrapheneOS
* Script ɗin zai sauke sabuwar sigar GrapheneOS don na'urar Pixel.
* Yana ƙirƙirar manyan fayiloli (directories) da ake bukata don adana fayilolin da aka sauke.
4. Tabbatar da sa hannu (signatures)
* Script ɗin zai sauke kuma ya tabbatar da masu sanya hannu waɗanda aka yarda da su don GrapheneOS don tabbatar da ingancin fayilolin shigarwa.
5. Sauke fayilolin shigarwa
* Script ɗin zai sauke fayilolin zip na shigarwa da sabuntawa na OTA don GrapheneOS.
6. Shiryawa na'urar
* Bi umarnin akan allo don buɗe bootloader na na'urar Pixel ɗinka.
7. Shigar GrapheneOS
* Script ɗin zai buɗe (unzip) fayilolin shigarwa kuma ya aiwatar da aikin flashing.
8. Kulle bootloader
* Bayan shigarwa, bootloader zai kasance an kulle shi don tsaro.


Muhimman bayanai

* Yi madadin bayananka:
Saka sabon tsarin aiki zai goge duk bayanai a na'urarka. Tabbatar ka adana duk muhimman bayanai.

* Tabbatar da boot:
Bayan shigarwa, yi amfani da fasahohin tabbatarwa da GrapheneOS ke bayarwa don duba ingancin shigarwar.


Kammalawa:

Wannan script ɗin yana sauƙaƙe shigar GrapheneOS a na'urar Pixel ɗinka. Ta bin matakan da aka bayyana a sama, za ka tabbatar da shigarwa mara matsala. Idan kana da wata matsala ko tambaya, duba takardun GrapheneOS na hukuma ko nemi taimako daga al'umma.
